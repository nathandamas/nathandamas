# About

Nathan Damas Antonio is a GIScience researcher with a background in Cartographic and Land Surveying Engineering, a master's degree in Geodetic Sciences, and ongoing doctoral research at the Federal University of Paraná.

> [Versão em português](../../pt/about/index.md)

## Extended academic bio

His trajectory combines academic research, technical consulting and the development of applied geospatial solutions. The submitted CVs depict a profile that moves consistently across **collaborative cartography, VGI, OpenStreetMap, urban mobility, spatial analysis, semantic interoperability, spatial data infrastructures, photogrammetry, LiDAR, UAV data and 3D cadastre**.

On the academic side, his recent work has focused on the spatio-temporal interpretation of OpenStreetMap contributions, the transparency and quality of collaborative geodata, and the use of computational methods to analyse, model and document geospatial processes. In parallel, he has been developing research that brings together natural language, conceptual models and geospatial practice, including applications of NLP, voice recognition and large language models.

On the technical and professional side, his experience includes geographic database modelling and structuring, workflow automation with Python and PostGIS, multi-source data integration, cartographic and semantic standardization, metadata management, interoperability of geospatial services and support for applied research in institutional environments. He also has a solid background in photogrammetry, LiDAR, UAV-based datasets, aerial mapping and three-dimensional territorial representation.

His academic and international trajectory includes PhD research at UFPR and a period as a visiting researcher at Heidelberg University, in addition to work as an interim teacher, geoprocessing consultant and university-lab researcher. This combination of research, technical development and applied practice defines a profile particularly well suited to GIScience, geospatial infrastructures and territorial data analysis.
