# Research

> [Versão em português](../../pt/research/index.md)

## Main research areas

### 1. Collaborative cartography, VGI and OpenStreetMap
Research on contribution, deletion, transparency and data-quality patterns in collaborative geodata, with emphasis on spatio-temporal interpretation and analytical reading of editing behaviour.

### 2. Spatial and spatio-temporal analysis
Development of quantitative and computational approaches to interpret territorial dynamics, urban mobility and phenomena observed in geospatial time series.

### 3. Semantic interoperability
Studies on integrating conceptual models, ontologies and heterogeneous geospatial data structures, including support from natural language processing and LLMs.

### 4. Spatial data infrastructures and automation
Spatial database modelling, geospatial ETL, metadata, service interoperability and workflow automation with Python, PostGIS and GIS tools.

### 5. Photogrammetry, LiDAR, UAV and 3D cadastre
Applications involving point-cloud integration, aerial mapping, orthophotos and three-dimensional representation of territorial objects.
