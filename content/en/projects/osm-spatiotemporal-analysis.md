# OpenStreetMap: spatio-temporal analysis of contributions and deletions

> [Versão em português](../../pt/projects/osm-spatiotemporal-analysis.md)

## Overview

This project brings together a research line focused on understanding editing dynamics in OpenStreetMap, with emphasis on spatio-temporal patterns, deletions, collaborative-base maturation and analytical visualization.

## Why it matters

A critical reading of OpenStreetMap's evolution is essential for discussing reliability, transparency, data quality and territorial behaviour in one of the world's largest collaborative geodata projects.

## Goals

- analyse OSM contributions and deletions;
- model temporal series of editing behaviour;
- compare patterns across grid cells and urban areas;
- support transparency and critical reading of collaborative data;
- produce dashboards and open-science outputs.

## Associated output

- *Temporal Dynamics of OpenStreetMap Contributions and Deletions in Curitiba*
- *Development of a dashboard for spatio-temporal analysis of OpenStreetMap edits in Brazil*
- *An AI-Based Approach To Identify Spatio-Temporal Contribution Patterns And Their Impact On OpenStreetMap Data Quality*
- *Transparency and Trust in Collaborative Mapping*
