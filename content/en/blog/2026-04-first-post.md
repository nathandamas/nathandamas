# First post on the website

This is the first text published in the website blog.

The purpose of this section is to gather reflections, technical notes, methodological
comments, and short texts connected to my research and development agenda in geospatial technologies.

## What I intend to publish here

- notes on OpenStreetMap and collaborative cartography;
- methodological comments on spatial and spatio-temporal analysis;
- texts on semantic interoperability;
- records of projects, tools, and technical lessons learned;
- reflections on GIScience, open science, and academic production.

## Why create this section

In addition to the static pages for curriculum, projects, and publications, the blog
makes it possible to register ongoing ideas, methodological observations, and more direct
comments on topics that cut across my research.

The goal is not to turn this page into a repository of long and generic texts, but into
a space for focused, clear, and continuous technical and academic writing.

## Upcoming topics

In the next posts, I intend to address topics such as:

- contribution and deletion dynamics in OpenStreetMap;
- spatio-temporal analysis applied to collaborative data;
- semantic interoperability between geospatial models;
- NLP and LLMs in geospatial contexts;
- experiences with spatial data infrastructures, spatial databases, and automation.
