# NLP and LLMs applied to the geospatial domain

> [Versão em português](../../pt/projects/nlp-llm-geospatial.md)

## Overview

This research strand connects natural language, conceptual models and geospatial practice, using natural language processing, voice recognition and large language models.

## Why it matters

Heterogeneous geospatial data and specialized workflows often face semantic, terminological and operational barriers. This strand investigates how language-based methods can support interpretation, validation and integration.

## Goals

- explore NLP use cases in geospatial contexts;
- test LLM applications for geospatial reasoning and practice;
- support semantic alignment and validation of heterogeneous structures;
- bridge natural language and specialized geospatial workflows.
